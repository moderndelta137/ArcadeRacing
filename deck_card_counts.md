## Archetype Design Notes

| Deck | Core plan | Speed / Gear target | Main synergy | Built-in weakness |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Balance Set / Bright Red AE86 | Flexible starter tempo with acceleration, braking, drift, lane change, discard, and extra-card sequencing. | 50-80 km/h / 3rd Gear. | Small corrections chain into clean corner tempo. | No extreme power spike. |
| RWD Drift Set / Bright Green Lamborghini Huracan | Hold high speed, use Turn cards to pass checks, then gain speed after cornering. | Lower 4th Gear comfort band. | Drift setup plus After payoff. | Weak lane change and opponent disruption. |
| AWD Grip Set / Yellow Porsche 911 | Adapt through choice-based Drive effects and gradual speed management. | Balanced Gear spread. | Inner-lane control, understeer counters, and micro-correction. | Low explosiveness and no drift-style speed-limit modification. |
| Muscle Car Set / Blue Mustang GT500 | Create immediate power and contact pressure. | Wide 5th Gear plus future 6th Gear. | Attack cards force speed loss, lane denial, movement, or collisions. | Poor braking, lane change, and fine control. |
